Lesson 1: What are indices?

Learning Outcome

Understand the meaning of an index and write repeated multiplication using index notation.

Key Idea

\(a^n\) means multiplying \(a\) by itself \(n\) times.

Worked Example

\(2^4 = 2 \times 2 \times 2 \times 2 = 16\)

Common Mistake

Mistake: \(2^4 = 2 \times 4\)

Fix: The index tells you how many times the base is multiplied by itself.

Mini Practice
