Lesson 1: Introduction to Linear Equations

Learning Outcome

Understand and solve equations of the form \(x + a = b\).

Key Idea

An equation stays balanced if you do the same operation to both sides.

Worked Example

Solve \(x + 5 = 12\).

  1. Subtract 5 from both sides
  2. \(x = 7\)

Common Mistake

Mistake: Adding 5 instead of subtracting.

Mini Practice

Solve \(x - 3 = 10\)

Answer: 13
